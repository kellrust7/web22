Checkpoint 1 - 3 Ideas



Topic 1: Outdoor/Camping Gear

This company will be similar to something like Patagonia, making outdoor gear and accessories. There will be more of a focus on its local origins.


Topic 2: Florist: Anomaly Forals

A florist that uses rare or unconventional plants. These are usually imported from other areas of the world and are a unique twist on typical floral arrangements. They provide for weddings, parties, and other events with the desire for more conversational decor.


Topic 3: Outdoor Living/Furniture

A company that sells outdoor furniture, games, decor, lighting, etc. This company is all about creating the perfect outdoor atmosphere.
